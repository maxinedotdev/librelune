Librelune is a privacy-respecting moon phase widget for Android.

It shows the current lunar phase, illumination, age, and a realistic orientation wobble that changes with location.

The widget is designed to be lightweight, offline-capable, and free of tracking.

Features:
- Accurate moon phase and illumination display
- Optional realistic wobble/orientation rendering
- Multiple widget styles
- No ads, no analytics, no proprietary tracking SDKs
- Fully open source (AGPL-3.0)
